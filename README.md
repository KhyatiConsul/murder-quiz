# If You Were the Killer…

> *"Don't think too hard. Answer honestly. The shadows are listening."*

A dark psychological quiz that reveals what kind of killer you'd be — based on motive, method, and instinct.

---

## What is this?

Seven questions. No right answers. Each choice reveals something about how you think, feel, and react under pressure.

At the end, you get your archetype — a psychological portrait built from your answers.

**Try it →** [murder-quiz.vercel.app](https://murder-quiz.vercel.app)
